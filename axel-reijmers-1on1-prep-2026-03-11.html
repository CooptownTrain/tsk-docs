Conversation Guide

Assess whether Axel is a competent financial operator or a placeholder. Build the working relationship. Come away understanding his actual role and the raise timeline.

Opener (2 min)

Ask about Japan. Was it personal or TSK business? Establishes rapport and may reveal whether he's doing deal work abroad.

Topic 1: His Role & Engagement (5 min)

  • Are you full-time on this or splitting time with Castle Placement and other things?

Why: Establishes the baseline. You need to know if he's in the same boat as you (contingent on raise) or operating differently.

Topic 2: DealMaker / Reg CF Raise (8 min)

Topic 3: Financial Model & Projections (5 min)

  • What's the use-of-proceeds breakdown you're building for Reg CF?

Why: This is where you find out if Axel is a real CFO. If he has a model, ask to see it. If he doesn't, that's a major gap.

Topic 4: Partnerships & Revenue (5 min)
