Make sure the DealMaker process runs smoothly. Keep the people responsible for deliverables engaged, motivated, and informed on process objectives and deliverables.

Translation: Steve is the process manager / deliverable traffic controller for the raise. Not strategy, not investor relations — execution coordination.

K's $20M target requires both phases. The $5M Reg CF aligns with Steve's Effective Date trigger. Reg A+ would be a separate process entirely.
