4. Pattern Analysis

1. The Accountability Gap

Action items assigned in every meeting. Most reappear unfinished the next time. Same tasks show up 2, 3, 4 times. Nathan tracked them but had no authority to enforce. K gave direction but didn't hold individuals accountable between meetings.

2. Capital as Universal Blocker

Nearly every initiative stalls at "waiting for budget" or "after the raise." This has been true since June 2025. Nine months of stall. The DealMaker process itself required external capital ($100K from Steven) just to start.

3. Data Room Never Built

Discussed July 2025. Folders created. K was supposed to populate. Still empty. A fundamental investor readiness failure.

4. DealMaker Timeline Slippage

Oct 14: "start end of this week." Dec 9: signing by Dec 11. Jan 6: "start in early Feb." Mar 2026: still onboarding. Five months of drift.

5. Key People Going Dark

Sam (CRO): stopped engaging Aug 2025. John (GC): no-shows from Sep 2025. Jalyn (IP): left after two weeks. No exit explanations for any. K's approach: replace, don't investigate.

6. Revenue Model Unresolved

Oct 28 notes lay out two options (lead gen vs operator). Nathan flagged both are hard. Can't build financial model, sales strategy, or platform without answering it. Robert's strategy introduces a third framing (intermediary-led proof). Needs resolution before the raise.
